Recipe:

1. Cut and peel avocados
2. Scoop avocados into a bowl
3. Mash avocados
4. Dice tomatoes and onions
5. Add diced tomatoes and onions to the mashed avocados
6. Cut limes in half and use juicer to juice the limes 
7. Add lime juice to the bowl
8. Finely chop cilantro and add to the bowl
9. Add salt to taste
10. Eat!
